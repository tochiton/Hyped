Duck's 5050 Raffle

Inspired by team of The Trail Blazers Foundation and Safeway/Albertsons Foundation, UO has also started up with 5050 Central to offer the make it better Duck's 5050 Raffle in partnership with the Safeway/Albertsons Foundation during the 2016-17 NCAA Football season.

Fans purchased raffle tickets at home games for a chance to win half of the net proceeds raised for that game through two in-arena kiosks and mobile sellers throughout the arena.
